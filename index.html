Meet TWICE!

TWICE is a South korean pop group made of 9 members: Jihyo, Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Mina, Sana, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu.
TWICE debuted in Oct, 2015 through a survival show under JYP entertainment. TWICE is known for their catchy and upbeat pop music. TWICE performances are very energenic.

TWICE has gained immense popularity in international countries. TWICE's dedicated fanbase is refered to as "ONCE"
